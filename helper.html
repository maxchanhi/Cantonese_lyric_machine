For this helper, the notation of the tones is based on Yale or Jyutping tone number system.The solfege is suggestive and can be transposed to different pitches. This web tool is useful for applying pitches to text.

As Cantonese is a tonal language, when setting text to melody, it is important to consider the pitch contour of each syllable. Cantonese has six phonemic tones (1-6), each corresponding to a different pitch contour. Additionally, there are three "checked tones" (which are pronounced shortly) based on the pitch of the first, third, and sixth phonemic tones. These are usually written as 7-9, respectively. It is crucial to accurately convey these pitch contours in order to properly communicate in Cantonese.
